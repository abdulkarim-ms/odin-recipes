Magarita

home

Description

ingredient

  • 1 cup ice
  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1 oz triple sec
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • ½ tsp tajín or chili powder (for rim)

Steps

  1. Rim glass with lime + tajín.
  2. Blend ice, tequila, triple sec, lime juice, and fruit (if using) until slushy
  3. Pour into the glass and add a spicy straw.
